Pat's Attitudes Towards Tinkering
Pat will tinker and explore software features a bit, but if Pat doesn't think that they are getting closer to their goal, Pat will revert to familiar ways. When Pat sees a need to learn new technology, Pat does so by trying out new features or commands to see what they do and to understand how the software works. Pat does this purposefully; that is, they reflect on each bit of feedback they get along the way to understand how they might benefit from the feature. Eventually, if Pat doesn't think it will get them closer to what they want to achieve, they will revert back to ways that they already know will work.
